# Cerberus Enterprise — Master Services Agreement (MSA)

**Effective Date:** March 10, 2026
**Version:** 1.0

This Master Services Agreement ("Agreement") is entered into by and between **Six Sense Enterprise Services LLC**, a Georgia limited liability company ("Provider"), and the entity identified in the Order Form ("Customer").

---

## 1. SCOPE

**1.1** This Agreement governs Customer's purchase and use of Cerberus Enterprise products and services, as specified in one or more Order Forms executed by both parties.

**1.2** Each Order Form is incorporated into and governed by this Agreement. In the event of a conflict between this Agreement and an Order Form, the Order Form prevails for that specific order.

**1.3** The End User License Agreement (EULA) governs software licensing terms. The Service Level Agreement (SLA) governs support and availability commitments. Both are incorporated by reference.

---

## 2. SERVICES

**2.1 Software License.** Provider grants Customer a license to use Cerberus Enterprise software as specified in the Order Form and subject to the EULA.

**2.2 Support Services.** Provider provides technical support during the Term as specified in the SLA.

**2.3 Professional Services.** If specified in an Order Form, Provider may deliver professional services including: deployment assistance, custom configuration, security assessment integration, and training. Professional services are scoped in a Statement of Work (SOW) attached to the Order Form.

**2.4 Updates.** Provider delivers software updates, patches, and new versions during the Term at no additional cost.

---

## 3. ORDER FORMS

**3.1 Content.** Each Order Form specifies: (a) products and services ordered; (b) license type (per-instance, per-organization); (c) number of licensed Instances; (d) fees and payment schedule; (e) Term; and (f) any special terms.

**3.2 Execution.** Order Forms are effective when signed by authorized representatives of both parties. Electronic signatures are acceptable.

**3.3 Changes.** Changes to an Order Form require a written amendment signed by both parties.

---

## 4. FEES AND PAYMENT

**4.1 Fees.** Customer pays the fees specified in each Order Form. Unless otherwise stated, fees are annual and invoiced at the start of each Term.

**4.2 Payment Terms.** Invoices are due net thirty (30) days from invoice date unless specified otherwise in the Order Form.

**4.3 Late Payment.** Overdue amounts accrue interest at 1.5% per month (or the maximum legal rate, whichever is less). Provider may suspend services if payment is more than thirty (30) days overdue after written notice.

**4.4 Taxes.** All fees are exclusive of taxes. Customer pays all applicable sales, use, VAT, and similar taxes, except taxes on Provider's net income.

**4.5 Price Changes.** Provider may adjust pricing for renewal Terms with at least sixty (60) days written notice before the renewal date.

---

## 5. TERM AND TERMINATION

**5.1 Agreement Term.** This Agreement begins on the Effective Date and continues until all Order Forms have expired or been terminated.

**5.2 Order Form Term.** Each Order Form has its own Term, typically twelve (12) months, with automatic annual renewal unless either party provides thirty (30) days written notice of non-renewal.

**5.3 Termination for Cause.** Either party may terminate this Agreement or any Order Form if the other party: (a) materially breaches and fails to cure within thirty (30) days of notice; or (b) becomes insolvent, files for bankruptcy, or ceases operations.

**5.4 Termination for Convenience.** Customer may terminate an Order Form for convenience with sixty (60) days written notice. No refund of prepaid fees is provided for early termination.

**5.5 Effect of Termination.** Upon termination: (a) Customer's license rights terminate; (b) Customer must destroy all copies of the Software; (c) Provider invoices for any unpaid fees through the termination date; (d) Sections 4, 6, 7, 8, 9, and 10 survive.
